Hollow Cheeks (Ice Technique)

The cold-exposure facial sculpting technique that creates defined cheekbones and the coveted hollow cheek look.

The Science

Rubbing ice on the inside of the cheeks can stimulate vasoconstriction and increase local fat metabolism. While not a magic fix, cold exposure may improve cheek definition over time. This mimics some effects of buccal fat reduction in a safer, non-invasive way.

Protocol

Rub an ice cube on the inner cheek lining for 3–5 minutes, once or twice daily.

  • Use a clean ice cube (made from filtered water)
  • Focus on the middle and lower part of the inner cheek
  • Apply gentle pressure as you move the ice in circular motions
  • Perform for 3-5 minutes per side, once or twice daily

Enhanced Protocol

For accelerated results, combine the ice method with:

  • Gua sha facial massage to improve lymphatic drainage
  • Reduced sodium intake to minimize water retention
  • Chewing exercises to develop the masseter muscles

Hormones & Growth Optimization

Boosting testosterone and growth hormone naturally requires a foundation of proper nutrition, rest, and exercise.

Optimized Nutrition Protocol

  • Protein: Aim for 100g/day, focusing on meat, eggs, and dairy.
  • Carbs & Sugars: Include simple sugars (e.g. fruits, raw honey) post-workout to spike insulin and raise IGF-1.
  • Milk: Rich in calcium and casein protein, supports muscle and bone health.

Note: A moderate approach of 100–150g of carbs spread through the day, paired with physical training, will promote IGF-1 safely.

Cortisol Management

High cortisol is linked to insulin resistance and can inhibit testosterone production. Manage stress with:

  • Proper sleep (7-9 hours in a dark room)
  • Morning light exposure (15-30 minutes)
  • Adaptogens like ashwagandha (300-600mg daily)

Training for Hormonal Response

Certain exercise protocols maximize natural testosterone and growth hormone production:

  • Compound lifts (squats, deadlifts, bench press)
  • Sprint intervals (30 seconds on, 90 seconds rest)
  • Training in a fasted state occasionally

Facial Structure Enhancement

Evidence-supported methods for facial definition that reshape and enhance your natural facial structure.

Mewing Technique

Proper tongue posture improves facial definition over time by reshaping the maxilla and midface.

  • Keep the entire tongue pressed to the roof of your mouth
  • Maintain lips sealed and breathe through your nose
  • Practice proper swallowing (without using cheek muscles)
  • Maintain this position as much as possible, especially while sleeping

Hard Chewing

Use mastic gum or raw vegetables to strengthen the masseter muscles and create a wider jaw appearance.

  • Chew mastic gum for 15-30 minutes daily
  • Focus on using the back molars
  • Alternate sides to ensure balanced development

Neck and Posture Training

Chin tucks, neck bridges, and posture correction enhance your overall facial appearance by creating proper alignment.

  • Perform 3 sets of 10 chin tucks daily
  • Practice good posture with shoulders back and head aligned
  • Sleep on your back without a pillow or with a thin one

Bonus: Gua sha or facial massages help with lymphatic drainage and circulation, contributing to tighter, fresher-looking skin.

Height Maximization Protocol

Our advanced height enhancement techniques can add noticeable inches to your perceived and actual height.

Spinal Decompression

The spine naturally compresses throughout the day. These techniques can help you regain and maximize your full height:

  • Hanging exercises: Hang from a pull-up bar for 30-60 seconds, 3-5 sets daily
  • Inversion table: Use for 5-10 minutes daily to reverse spinal compression
  • Child's pose stretch: Hold for 1-2 minutes before bed

Growth Stimulation

While most height increase occurs during puberty, these methods may help late bloomers and support maximum height expression:

  • Deep sleep optimization (7-9 hours in complete darkness)
  • Sprint training to increase growth hormone production
  • Zinc supplementation (15-30mg daily with food)
  • Vitamin D3 (2000-5000 IU daily) with K2 (100mcg)

Postural Enhancement

Proper posture can instantly add 1-2 inches to your perceived height:

  • Wall exercises: Stand against wall with heels, buttocks, shoulders and head touching
  • Strengthen core muscles with planks and hollow holds
  • Stretch hip flexors daily to prevent anterior pelvic tilt

Frame & Shoulder Development

Widening your frame naturally requires smart training and volume to create an impressive V-taper.

Key Exercises

  • Lateral Raises: 3–5 sets, 10-15 reps, 3–4 times per week
  • Wide Grip Pull-ups: 3-4 sets to near failure, 2-3 times weekly
  • Face Pulls: 3 sets of 15-20 reps, focus on external rotation
  • Rear Delt Flies: 3 sets of 12-15 reps with light to moderate weight

Postural Realignment

Include these exercises to open the chest and reset rounded shoulders:

  • Thoracic extensions over a foam roller
  • Doorway stretches for chest and anterior delts
  • Band pull-aparts, 3 sets of 20 reps daily

Advanced Techniques

For maximum frame development, incorporate these strategies:

  • Progressive overload: Increase weight or reps consistently
  • Mind-muscle connection: Focus intensely on the targeted muscles
  • Time under tension: Use slower negatives (3-4 seconds down)
  • Adequate recovery: 48 hours between training the same muscle groups

Lymphatic Drainage & Facial Health

NASA-researched rebounding techniques to improve lymphatic flow, reduce puffiness and support detoxification.

Rebounding Protocol

NASA found that rebounding (jumping up and down) improves lymphatic flow more effectively than other forms of exercise:

  • Try 5–10 minutes of trampoline or light jump rope per day
  • Start with 2-minute sessions if you're a beginner, working up to 10 minutes
  • For best results, perform in the morning on an empty stomach

Facial Lymphatic Massage

Complement rebounding with these targeted massage techniques:

  • Use a gua sha stone to stroke upward and outward from the center of the face
  • Focus on jaw, cheekbones, and under-eye area
  • Perform for 3-5 minutes daily after applying facial oil

Hydration Strategy

Proper hydration is essential for lymphatic health:

  • Drink 3-4 liters of clean water daily
  • Consider adding electrolytes to maximize cellular hydration
  • Reduce salt intake to minimize water retention

Q: How long until I see results?

A: Start with the methods that address your primary concerns, then gradually incorporate others. The protocols work synergistically but can be effective individually.

Q: Are these methods safe?

A: All protocols are designed to be natural and non-invasive. However, consult with a healthcare professional before beginning any new supplement regimen, especially if you have existing health conditions.

Protocol-Specific Questions

Q: Is mewing effective for adults?

A: While mewing has the most dramatic effects during developmental years, adults can still achieve noticeable improvements in facial structure and definition with consistent practice over time.

Q: How much can height really be increased?

Q: Which supplements are most important?
